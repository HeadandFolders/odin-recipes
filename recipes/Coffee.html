Coffee Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp Nestle sweetened condensed milk
  • 3/4 tbsp Instant Coffee
  • 1 tbsp Coffee Mate
  • 1/2 cup of water

Steps

  1. Pour the condensed milk into your cup (it should kinda barely cover the entire bottom of your cup)
  2. Bring 1/2 cup of water to a boil
  3. Add the coffee and coffee mate to your cup
  4. Pour a bit of water. Just enough enough to dissolve the stuff in the cup
  5. Pour boiling water in the cup.
